About Prime Cyprus

A relocation partner built for the digital economy.

Prime Cyprus exists to help founders, investors, and high‑leverage operators move their lives and structures to a jurisdiction that actually understands digital wealth.

Our Story

Born from the pain of building in high‑tax, low‑clarity systems.

Prime Cyprus was founded by operators and investors who spent years building in jurisdictions where the rules were written for an industrial economy, not a digital one. We saw talented people punished for volatility, innovation, and cross‑border income — and we realized there was a better way.

Cyprus offered the ingredients we were looking for: EU rule of law, a transparent tax code, and a regime that treats software, IP, and crypto as first‑class citizens. Prime Cyprus was created to bridge that opportunity to the builders who need it most.

From founders, for founders

We started as founders relocating our own companies and portfolios to Cyprus. Every structure we propose is one we would be comfortable using ourselves.

Global, remote‑first clients

Our clients live in airports and on Zoom calls — not in one office. We build plans around remote income, distributed teams, and digital assets.

On‑the‑ground execution

From bank appointments to property inspections, we work with vetted local professionals in Cyprus so your plan is not just optimized on paper, but implemented in the real world.

Why We're Different

More than a real estate agent. More than a law firm.

Generic agents sell you whatever they have on inventory. We start with your tax position, residency strategy, and long‑term plan — then design property and company structures around that.

Prime Cyprus vs. traditional real estate

  • ✓ Strategy‑first, property‑second — we never start by sending you a folder of listings. We begin with tax, residency, and lifestyle goals, then match assets that serve the plan.
